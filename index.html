AI Acceptance & Trust
Please read the concept description below before answering. There are no right answers — your honest reaction is what matters.

About MedVision: MedVision is an AI-powered platform that allows you to upload X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and other medical images. The AI analyzes them and returns possible findings, highlighted regions of concern, confidence scores, and a draft radiology report — in under 2 minutes. The system is designed to assist your clinical judgment, not replace it. Your professional decision always takes precedence.
